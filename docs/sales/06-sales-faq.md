## Product Questions

### What is MyLeadX.ai?
MyLeadX.ai is an AI-powered lead management platform that combines CRM, AI voice agents, and multi-channel communication (WhatsApp, Email, SMS) to help Indian businesses capture, nurture, and convert leads automatically.

### How is it different from a regular CRM?
Unlike traditional CRMs that only store data, MyLeadX.ai actively engages leads with AI voice agents that can call, qualify, and schedule appointments in 8+ Indian languages - 24/7, without human intervention.

### What industries does it support?

- Healthcare (appointments, consultations)
- Insurance (policies, claims)
- Automotive (test drives, sales)
- IT Services (projects, proposals)
- Recruitment (hiring, interviews)
- Finance (loans, investments)
- E-commerce (support, orders)

## Technical Questions

### How does the AI voice agent work?
Our AI agents use advanced speech recognition and natural language processing to have human-like conversations. They can understand context, answer questions, handle objections, and take actions like scheduling appointments or transferring to humans.

### What's the call quality like?
We use premium voice providers (ElevenLabs, AI4Bharat) with less than 500ms latency. Calls sound natural and professional, not robotic.

### Can AI agents transfer calls to humans?
Yes! AI agents can intelligently transfer calls to human agents when needed. You can define transfer conditions based on lead questions, sentiment, or specific triggers.

### Does it integrate with our existing systems?
Yes, we offer:
- REST API for custom integrations
- Webhooks for real-time events
- Zapier for 5000+ app connections
- Native integrations with Facebook, Google, WhatsApp, Razorpay, etc.

### Is the data secure?
Absolutely. We provide:
- SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
- End-to-end encryption
- Data stored in India
- Role-based access control
- Complete audit trails
- GDPR compliance

## Common Objections & Responses

### "We already have a CRM"
"Great! MyLeadX.ai can work alongside your existing CRM. Many customers use us specifically for AI calling while keeping their current CRM for record-keeping. We also offer migration support if you want to consolidate."

### "AI can't replace human callers"
"You're right - and that's not our goal. AI agents handle the high-volume, repetitive tasks (initial outreach, qualification, scheduling) so your human team can focus on high-value conversations. It's augmentation, not replacement."

### "Our customers prefer talking to humans"
"Most customers can't tell they're talking to AI - our agents are that natural. And AI ensures instant response (no waiting on hold), 24/7 availability, and consistent quality. For customers who request humans, AI seamlessly transfers them."

### "It's too expensive for a startup"
"Our Starter plan at Rs 1,999/month is less than the cost of one part-time telecaller. Plus, startups get 50% off the first year. The ROI is typically positive within the first month."

### "We need to think about it"
"I understand. How about starting with our free 14-day trial? You'll get full access to all features, and our team will help you set up your first AI agent. There's no commitment - you can decide after seeing it in action."

### "We're worried about compliance"
"Compliance is built into our platform:

We help you stay compliant automatically."
