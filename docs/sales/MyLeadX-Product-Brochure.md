## The Business Challenge

Indian businesses face critical challenges in lead management:

- **Delayed Response**: Average lead response time exceeds 24 hours, resulting in 78% lead wastage
- **High Operational Costs**: Telecalling teams require significant investment in hiring, training, and infrastructure
- **Language Barriers**: India's linguistic diversity makes scaling customer communication complex
- **Fragmented Tools**: Multiple disconnected systems for calling, messaging, and CRM create operational inefficiencies
- **Limited Visibility**: Lack of real-time analytics hampers data-driven decision making
