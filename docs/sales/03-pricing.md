## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a Voice Minute?
A voice minute is calculated as one minute of AI voice agent conversation. Both inbound and outbound calls consume voice minutes.

### Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?
You'll receive notifications as you approach limits. You can purchase add-ons or upgrade your plan. We never cut off your service without warning.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any plan. Enterprise customers may have implementation fees for custom development.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. No questions asked.

### Can I try before I buy?
Yes! Our Free Trial gives you 14 days to explore the platform with full features.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, are SOC 2 compliant, and follow GDPR and Indian data protection regulations.
