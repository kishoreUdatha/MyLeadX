# MyLeadX Pricing Strategy
## Executive Summary

**Document Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** April 2025 | **Confidential**

---

## Business Model Overview

MyLeadX operates on a **Hybrid Pricing Model** combining predictable subscription revenue with usage-based billing.

| Component | Type | Revenue Share |
|-----------|------|---------------|
| **Subscription** | Fixed Monthly | 82% of revenue |
| **Wallet (Usage)** | Pay-As-You-Go | 18% of revenue |
