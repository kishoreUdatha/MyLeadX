## Frequently Asked Questions

**What are AI Voice Minutes?**
AI Voice Minutes represent the duration of calls handled by AI agents. Both inbound and outbound AI calls consume minutes. Human agent calls do not count against your quota.

**Which Indian languages are supported?**
Growth plans and above include: Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese, and Urdu.

**Can I change plans?**
Yes. Upgrades are effective immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**What happens if I exceed my limits?**
You'll receive notifications at 80% and 100% usage. You can purchase add-on packs or upgrade your plan. Services continue uninterrupted.

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full features. No credit card required to start.
