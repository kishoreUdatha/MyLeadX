### "We already use [Competitor CRM]"
**Response**: "MyLeadX.ai can integrate with your existing CRM or replace it entirely. Our AI voice agents can call leads from any system. Many customers start by using MyLeadX for AI calling and gradually migrate their entire workflow."

### "AI voice isn't ready for Indian accents/languages"
**Response**: "We've optimized our AI specifically for Indian languages using AI4Bharat and Sarvam AI. Our agents handle Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and 5 other languages with native-level accuracy. Let me show you a demo."

### "We have our own telecalling team"
**Response**: "AI agents don't replace your team - they amplify them. Use AI for initial outreach and qualification, then transfer hot leads to your best closers. Most customers see 70% cost reduction while their team focuses on high-value conversations."

**Response**: "Consider the total cost: a single telecaller costs Rs 20,000+/month in salary alone. Our Starter plan at Rs 1,999 gives you 5 AI agents that work 24/7 without breaks. That's 10x more capacity at 1/10th the cost."
